Hands On Hoops Mission!

Hands On Hoops is a program that teaches kids, ages 2+, basketball and transferable skills such as being a great teammate, learning through having fun, building a positive attitude, listening/applying instructions and core basketball skills on adjustable baskets.

Every child will have an opportunity to grow at their own pace. They are also encouraged to build their leadership skills by applying their knowledge and teaching other kids in the class. Programming includes: 🏀Private group classes planned by parents (with complimentary classes) 🏀Basketball birthday parties 🏀One-on-one classes 🏀Public group classes 🏀In-school classes 🏀After school classes 🏀Adult skill-building classes 🏀Virtual classes
